Red Iguana Restaurant and Distance

Does is make the heart grow fonder?

Map authored by Ray Hasenyager

The map allows users to view distances from the Red Iguana Restaurant in Salt Lake City (40.7718, -111.9124). When the user clicks on the map they will see the distance from the selected point and the distance from the southwest map boundary of the re-centered map to the restaurant through the console log. The map also centers and zooms to level 6 at the selected location. The user can click on additional locations.
